Leviticus 26:35.3

Missing dagesh on shin; add note 'c'.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

The LC has no dagesh on the shin. The usual marking has a dagesh on the shin as in the Miqra according to the Masorah (MAM).

The UXLC transcribes the LC correctly, however.
