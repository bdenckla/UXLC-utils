Job 6:29.7

Examine accent under shin. Change merkha to meteg. Add note 'm'.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

This is the qere word of a qere/ketiv pair. The accent under the shin is too vertical to be considered a merkha here. Change merkha to meteg and add note 'm'.

BHL has a merkha under the shin; otherwise, it agrees with BHL. BHLA has no entry for this verse.
