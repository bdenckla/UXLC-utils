Job 17:3.3

Examine vowel under the ayin: patah, qamats. Change qamats to patah and add note 't'.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

The ayin has a very dark and clear horizontal bar sufficient for either a patah or qamats. A faint, but well positioned dot is below the bar, sufficient to be a qamats. Change qamats to patah and add transcription note 't'.

BHL has a qamats under the ayin. BHLA has no entry for this verse.
