Job 19:5.7

* Examine tav for dagesh (and rafe).
Credit: Sefaria.org.

A mis-shapen blob occurs in the resh that might be taken as a dagesh; it will be ignored. The dagesh in the pe is small, but clear. The dagesh in the tav is unusually light, but well-formed and well-positioned. The rafe on the tav is very clear. (A letter should not have both a dagesh and a rafe.) Retain the dagesh in the tav and add transcription note 't'.

BHL has no dagesh in the tav or resh; it doesn't normally display rafe marks. BHLA has a dagesh and a rafe on the tav and no dagesh on the resh.
