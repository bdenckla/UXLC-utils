Job 24:21.1

* Examine vowel under ayin: tsere or segol. Add note 't'.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

The ayin has two very dark and well-positioned dots under the ayin. Under these two dots is a very faint, slightly off-centered dot. Retain the tsere and add transcription note 't'.

BHL has a segol under the ayin. BHLA has a tsere under the ayin and a question mark in parentheses, "(?)".
