Job 31:15.1

Examine he for geresh-muqdam accent; examine order of markings below the he. Add note 't' as geresh-muqdam is ill-formed.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

The image shows somewhat lighter, yet distinct, vertical straight line arising from the top front of the he. It has a very odd shape for a geresh-muqdam. The order of the markings below the he is sheva then meteg then patah. Add a note 't' indicating transcription problem with geresh-muqdam marking.

BHL has no geresh-muqdam on the he; the order of the markings below the he is sheva then meteg then patah. BHLA has no entry for this verse.
