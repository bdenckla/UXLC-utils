Job 36:29.7

Examine whether kaf has a meteg. Add a meteg under the kaf and a transcription note 't'.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

The text quality is not good. The meteg under the tav is very dark; the mark under the kaf is much lighter, as is the qamats under the kaf. Add a meteg under the kaf and add a transcription note 't'.

BHL has no meteg on the kaf. BHLA has a meteg on both the kaf and the tav.
