Job 22:24.5

Examine whether the second letter is a bet or a kaf. The dagesh in the initial vav is weak; add note 't'.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

The text is of poor quality around this word. The second letter has the lower near-horizontal line protruding forward of the vertical line. The dagesh on the initial vav is very small, add note 't' to the initial vav.

BHL has a bet as the second letter; it has a dagesh in the initial vav. BHLA has no entry for this verse.
