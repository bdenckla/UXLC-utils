Job 6:10.1

Examine the marking under the vav and for a maqaf at end of word. Change the mark under the vav to meteg. Add note 'm'.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

The line under the vav lacks the northeast-to-southwest inclination to be a merkha. It's also rather short for a merkha. Change to meteg and add transcription note 'm'. There is no maqaf at end of this word.

BHL has a merkha under the vav and no maqaf. BHLA has no entry for this verse.
