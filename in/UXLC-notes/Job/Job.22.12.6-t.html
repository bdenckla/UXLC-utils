Job 22:12.6

Examine accent on the resh: dehi or tipeha. Add note 't'.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

The mark under the resh could be either a dehi or tipeha deApproved on its position relative to the resh. Space was available for the scribe to make the mark more pre-positive; hence, the accent is taken as a tipeha. Add note 't'.

BHL has a tipeha under the resh. BHLA has no entry for this verse.
