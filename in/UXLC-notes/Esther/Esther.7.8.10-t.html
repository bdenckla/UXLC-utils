Esther 7:8.10

Based on color photos, a revia instead of a zaqef qatan is uncertain.

Credit: Sefaria.org.

Two dots over the pe might be taken as revias. One is almost touching the top of the pe, the other is above the gap between the pe and the lamed. The revia on top of the pe is small and faint. Compare with the revias in 7:7.10 immediately above it or in the same verse at 7:8.24.

The upper dot of the zaqaf qatan might be considered hidden by the munah in 7:7.6 immediately above it.

The JPS Tanakh has a zaqef qatan.
The "Miqra according to Masora" shows a revia.
