Jeremiah 44:19.14

Based on color photos, the dagesh in the final he is an artifact.

Credit: Sefaria.org.

The high resolution, color image of the page shows that the dagesh is light, redish, and likely to be an artifact. The lack of a dagesh on the consonant with a rafe agrees with usual Masoretic practice.

A previous change, 2020.10.19 - Changes.xml, 2020.07.16-1, was based on a low resolution, black and white image of the text and had placed a dagesh in the he.
