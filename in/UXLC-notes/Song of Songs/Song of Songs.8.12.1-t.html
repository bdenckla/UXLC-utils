Song of Songs 8:12.1

Based on color photos, the accent on the kaf should be a patah, not a qamatz.

A qamats on the kaf is generally accepted as anomalous spelling. However, viewed in black and white facsimiles it is distinct and well-formed.

The color facsimile provided by the author (above) shows the qamatz dot has a different color from the qamatz bar and is possibly an artifact. The folio page has a large splash mark in the center and the dot in the qamatz may be the result of a sub-droplet of the splash. The dot is also to the left of the center of the patah bar in contrast with dots on most other qamatzs on the page.

If the dot does not represent scribal intent, the qamats becomes a patah as shown in the UXLC.
