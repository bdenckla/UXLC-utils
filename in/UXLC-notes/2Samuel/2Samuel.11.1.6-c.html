Samuel_2 11:1.6

Vowels on the initial mem and following lamed differ from other texts. Add 'c' note after lamed.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

Other texts have a sheva on the mem and a qamats on the lamed.

BHL has a sheva on the mem and a qamats on the lamed. BHLA has a patah on the mem and a sheva on the lamed. Both BHL texts have a dagesh on the mem.
