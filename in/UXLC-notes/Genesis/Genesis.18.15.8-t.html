Genesis 18:15.8

Add merkha after segol under mem. Add note 't'.
Credit: Sefaria.org.

The ink is chipped in this area; however, the remnant of a mark remains following the segol. The mark has the northeast-to-southwest inclination of a merkha. Add transcription note 't'. This word appears in a response to Jason Hare's b-hebrew/Hebrew Bible posting "What about Ktiv / Kri ?" of 28 Sep 2024 by Ben Denckla on 29 Sep 2024.

BHL has no accent under the mem. BHLA has no entry for this verse.
