Haggai 1:10.5

In the LC the shin has no shin/sin dots.

Credit: Sefaria.org.

The "Miqra according to the Masorah" shows a shin dot on the shin.

The UXLC has 46 shin consonants that lack either shin or sin dots. Forty-three of these shin consonants are the second shin in the word "יששכר", yissakhar (The 5-th son of Leah), which traditionally has no shin/sin dots. The remaining 3 shins without either shin or sin dots are Deut 24:16.1, Isa 46:4.6, and Hag 1:10.5.

This is probably a scribal error. Note that the UXLC has 58,195 shins in its normal and qere tags. Of these shins, 3, representing 0.0052% of the total qere shins, are in error in this way.
