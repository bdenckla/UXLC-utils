The yatir marking for this word is in the right margin; it is a yatir yod. The word has 2 yods; neither is vowelized. The first yod in the word will be noted as a yatir here. A note 't' will be added to reflect this ambiguity.

BHL has a qere/ketiv pair for this word; the qere has a single unvowelized, unaccented yod after the shin. BHLA has no entry for this verse.
