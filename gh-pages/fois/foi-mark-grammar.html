Exclusions:

  • Extraordinary upper dots and lower dots are stripped before clusters are classified on this page.
  • The three dually-cantillated passages are excluded: Exodus 20, Deuteronomy 5, and Genesis 35:22.

Additional patterns treated as ordinary (after any optional shsi-dot and optional dms-r):

  • meteg, CGJ, vowel, optional pre-wm.
  • On lamed only:
    • pq-vowel, optional ba-is, CGJ, xs-vowel.
    • pq-vowel, xs-vowel, optional above-accent.
  • xataf, ZWJ, meteg, optional pre-wm.
  • On the first letter of a word only:
    • munaḥ, deḥi.
    • meteg, pre-wm.
    • geresh or gershayim, telisha gedolah.
    • geresh muqdam, revia.
  • On any letter: meteg, oleh.
