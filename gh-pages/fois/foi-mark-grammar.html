This page lists the weird Hebrew letter clusters: those that are neither ordinary nor treated as ordinary.

We define ordinary as follows:

itemmeaning
shsi-dotshin-dot or sin-dot
dmsdagesh/mapiq/shuruq-dot
vowel
aomaccent or meteg

Clusters that are not ordinary, but treated as ordinary are listed in mark grammar 2.

Exclusions:

  • Extraordinary upper dots and lower dots are stripped before clusters are classified on this page.
  • The three dually-cantillated passages are excluded: Exodus 20, Deuteronomy 5, and Genesis 35:22.

There are 1,200,055 included clusters; 1,198,122 are ordinary, 1,910 are treated as ordinary, and 23 are weird. Those 23 weird ones are listed below.
