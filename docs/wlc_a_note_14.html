at issue Englishadded dagesh to vav-qamats

The MPK has no letter to carry a dagesh for the qere’s vav-qamats.

Although it cannot carry the vav’s dagesh, the ל carries the vav’s qamats as well as its own sheva. Unlike the manuscript, our MPK shows that qamats on a dotted circle rather than on the ל.
