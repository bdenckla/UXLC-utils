The MPK has no letter to carry a dagesh for the qere’s ב. The MPK (points on no letters) is two qamats marks and (what we intepret as) pashta (not qadma).

There is also a dot near the pashta, which we ignore. I.e. we assume it is not ink, or in any case not intentional.
