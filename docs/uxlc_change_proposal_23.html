notes:noteThe manuscript’s pointed ketiv (MPK) is וַיִּאָׄלְד֧וּ. (We use אׄ (א with an extraordinary upper dot) to stand in for a blank space.)
notes:note:2The MPK has no letter to carry a dagesh for the qere’s וָּ.
notes:note:3Unlike the dagesh, the qamats does not need a letter to carry it; it is allowed to be an orphan. It appears between the ḥiriq and the sheva (of the yod and ל respectively). Unlike the manuscript, our MPK shows that orphan qamats on a אׄ (א with an extraordinary upper dot).
