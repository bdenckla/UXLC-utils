notes:noteThe manuscript’s pointed ketiv (MPK) is ◌ִתְחִלַּ֖ת.
notes:note:2The MPK has no letter to carry a dagesh for the qere’s ב.
notes:note:3Although it cannot carry ב’s dagesh, the initial ת could carry the ב’s ḥiriq as well as its own sheva. Unlike the manuscript, our MPK shows that ḥiriq on a dotted circle rather than floating out on its own.
