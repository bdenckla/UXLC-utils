notes:noteThe manuscript’s pointed ketiv (MPK) is וַאִׄתְגָּעַ֤שׁ. (We use אׄ (א with an extraordinary upper dot) to hold marks not associated with a parent letter.)
notes:note-2The MPK has no letter to carry a dagesh for the qere’s yod.
notes:note-3Unlike the dagesh, the ḥiriq does not need a letter to carry it; it is allowed to be an orphan. It appears between the pataḥ and the sheva (of the vav and ת respectively). Unlike the manuscript, our MPK shows that orphan ḥiriq on a אׄ (א with an extraordinary upper dot).
