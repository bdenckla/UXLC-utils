notes:noteThe manuscript’s pointed ketiv (MPK) is הִזְאְׄמִנְתּוּן֙. (We use אׄ (א with an extraordinary upper dot) to stand in for a blank space.)
notes:note-2The MPK has no letter to carry a dagesh for the qere’s ד.
notes:note-3Unlike the dagesh, the sheva does not need a letter to carry it; it is allowed to be an orphan. It appears between the sheva and the ḥiriq (of the ז and מ respectively). Unlike the manuscript, our MPK shows that orphan sheva on a אׄ (א with an extraordinary upper dot).
