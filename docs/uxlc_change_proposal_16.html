notes:noteThe manuscript’s pointed ketiv (MPK) is אְׄאֵ֣ׄאׄ. (We use אׄ (א with an extraordinary upper dot) to stand in for a blank space.)
notes:note-2The MPK has no letter to carry a dagesh for the qere’s ב. The MPK (points on no letters) is sheva, tsere, and munaḥ.
notes:note-3Why in the margin does it say not only «בני קר ולא כת» but also «בני ק»?
notes:note-4Although Dotan does not note this word, he notes that the next word has an unexpected dagesh in its ב. I.e., it unexpectedly starts with בּ not ב. i.e. בִנְיָמִ֔ן (Vinyamin) would be expected.
