notes:noteThe qere atom at issue is part of the qere compound עַל־כֵּ֥ן.
notes:note:2The manuscript’s pointed ketiv (MPK) is עַל אֵ֣ׄאׄ. (We use אׄ (א with an extraordinary upper dot) to stand in for a blank space.)
notes:note:3The MPK has no letter to carry a dagesh for the qere’s כ. The MPK (points on no letters) is tsere and merkha. Well really those points are under ל not under nothing.
notes:note:4Dotan notes that strictly speaking, the implied qere is עַל כֵ֥ן. I think what he finds notable here is the MPK’s lack of a maqaf. I do not think he finds the MPK’s lack of dagesh notable because as far as I know, such a dagesh is never present.
