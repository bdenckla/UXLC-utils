מִבִּנְיָמִ֗ין

מִבִּן־יָמִ֗ין

נְיָ

1s9:1

The MPK’s final nun of מבן does not carry a sheva for the qere’s (non-final) נ, perhaps because that would be illegal. The maqaf disappears when going from the MPK to the qere.

Dotan notes that strictly speaking, the implied qere is מִבִּניָמִ֗ין (no sheva under middle נ).
