The MPK’s ן in מבן does not carry a sheva for the qere’s נ, perhaps because that would be illegal.

The maqaf disappears from the MPK. It is unclear why the maqaf is supplied in the first place. Perhaps it is supplied because without it, מִבִּן would be illegal: a word without an accent.

Dotan notes that strictly speaking, the implied qere is מִבִּניָמִ֗ין (no sheva under middle נ).
