The MPK has no letter to carry a dagesh for the qere’s ד.

Although it cannot carry the ד’s dagesh, the מ carries the ד’s sheva as well as its own ḥiriq. Unlike the manuscript, our MPK shows that sheva on a dotted circle rather than on the מ.
